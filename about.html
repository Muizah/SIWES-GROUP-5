About Us

Anzel Order-n-Track is a company that is dedicated to transforming the way you experience food ordering by providing self-ordering kiosks. Our kiosks allow customers to order food and drinks quickly and easily, we also provide real-time inventory tracking so customers can see what food is available before they order.

Our vision is to make it easier, faster, and more convenient for people to order food. We believe that self-ordering kiosks are the future of food service, and we are committed to providing the best possible experience for our customers.

Our self-ordering kiosks are designed to be easy to use and understand. They feature a large touchscreen display, a simple user interface, and a variety of payment options. Our kiosks also integrate with our real-time inventory tracking system, so customers can see what food is available before they order.
