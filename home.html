About Us

Anzel Order-n-Track is a company that is dedicated to transforming the way you experience food ordering by providing automated food counters. Our kiosks allow customers to order food and drinks quickly and easily, we also provide real-time inventory tracking so customers can see what food is available before they order.

Our vision is to make it easier, faster, and more convenient for people to order food. We believe that automated food counters are the future of food service, and we are committed to providing the best possible experience for our customers.

Our automated food counters are designed to be easy to use and understand. They feature a simple user interface, and a variety of payment options. Our kiosks also integrate with our real-time inventory tracking system, so customers can see what items are available before they order.

Our automated food counters are easy to integrate with any business, whether it is a large scale business or a small scale business.
